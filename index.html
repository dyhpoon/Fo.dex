What is Fo.dex?

Fo.dex is an android client application allows you to index your photos.

It is also fully open source under Apache v2.0 license on Github.

Why?

There is no special reason why we made this app, we made this app just for fun.

Will there be an IOS version?

No, we do not currently have plans for IOS version.
